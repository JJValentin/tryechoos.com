# GTM OS Landing Page Overhaul

Based on the feedback, the previous changes shifted the product narrative but completely lost the high-converting, outcome-driven SaaS aesthetic. We introduced "blobs of text," drifted into "feature selling," and missed opportunities to visually reinforce the GTM OS positioning (e.g., missing logos).

This plan outlines a complete section-by-section rebuild of `index.html` starting from the `index (live copy).html` baseline, leveraging the `ui/ux pro max` skill constraints.

## User Review Required

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Please review the section-by-section copy shifts below. The primary goal is to ensure we are **selling the OUTCOME of a GTM OS** (revenue, time saved, autonomous operation) rather than the **FEATURES** (cron jobs, API endpoints, wrapper specifics).

---

## Proposed Changes (Section by Section)

### 1. The Hero Section (Outcome & Integration Proof)
*   **Headline Pivot:** "Hire your first AI Content Team" ➔ `"Hire your first AI Go-To-Market Team."` or `"Revenue Operations. On Autopilot."`
*   **Subheadline:** Keep it punchy. `"Not a chatbot. An isolated team of 6 agents that monitors market intent, drafts in your voice, and runs your daily growth operations. While you sleep."`
*   **[NEW] Logo Cloud UI:** Underneath the CTA, we will add an elegant, faded logo cloud using Lucide icons to prove the "OS" nature immediately: 
    *   *Natively integrates with: [Gmail] [Calendar] [Docs] [Twitter/X] [Reddit]*

### 2. The Moat (Bento Box - Replace Features with Outcomes)
*   Instead of my previous text-heavy "Deep Operations" block, we will refine the 3 Bento pillars to sell GTM logic.
*   **Pillar 1:** `Autonomous GTM Engine` ("Scans X and Reddit for buying signals while you sleep.")
*   **Pillar 2:** `Compounding Intelligence` ("Permanently fixes errors after 3 corrections.")
*   **Pillar 3:** `Native Workspace Action` ("Drafts straight into Google Docs. Books straight to Calendar. No copy-pasting.")

### 3. The Pipeline (5-Step Loop)
*   Keep the visual connecting line, but tighten the text drastically to prevent "blobs".
*   1. **Market Intake:** Scout scrapes intent.
*   2. **Strategy Alignment:** Sofi maps to your offer.
*   3. **Voice Drafting:** Echo writes the outbound replies.
*   4. **System Ops:** Rex logs it in your workspace.
*   5. **CAIS Upgrade:** The system learns from your approval.

### 4. The Agent Roster (Visual Cleanup)
*   The original copy was good, but we need to ensure the agents sound like a GTM team, not just content.
*   Will use short, punchy `[Outcome] + [Mechanism]` bullet formats so the UI doesn't turn into a wall of text.

### 5. Section 7: "The Real Cost of GTM" (Total Redo)
*   We are pivoting from "The Real Cost of Content" ($4,500/mo) to "The Real Cost of Fragmented GTM".
*   **Old Comparison:** Freelance Writer, Research Consultant, Jasper Tool Stack.
*   **New Comparison:** 
    *   `Growth Agency / RevOps ($2,500)`
    *   `SDR / Lead Gen Tooling ($1,500)`
    *   `Fragmented AI Subscriptions ($500)`
*   **Contrast:** Versus `BusinessOS ($97/mo)` - saving ~$4,400 with a unified, autonomous system.

### 6. Delete Section 6.5 (The Feature List)
*   The feature-selling "Deep Infrastructure" section I added will be entirely deleted. The "OS" nature will be proven visually via the new Hero Logo Cloud and the tight Bento Box outcomes.
