Common Questions

How does voice training actually work?
When you start, Zara walks you through a 20-minute onboarding conversation. She asks about your communication style, the topics you care about, your audience, and how you naturally express ideas. This creates your Voice Profile: a 14-dimension map covering tone, rhythm, vocabulary, perspective, and more. Echo uses this profile every time she writes, and self-grades each draft before delivery. The more you interact and give feedback, the more calibrated your team becomes.
What kind of content does EchoOS produce?
EchoOS creates long-form LinkedIn posts, Twitter/X threads, newsletter sections, blog drafts, and short-form social copy. The pipeline draws from content seeds you plant (ideas, angles, themes) and Sofi's strategic direction. You get ready-to-review drafts every weekday, calibrated to your voice and goals.
Is my content and data kept private?
Yes. Every customer gets their own isolated container with a dedicated agent instance. Your voice profile, content seeds, drafts, and workspace data are never shared with other customers or used to train shared models. You own your data, and you can export it at any time.
Do I need any technical setup or integrations?
No technical setup required. You sign up, go through voice onboarding with Zara, and your pipeline starts running. Optionally, you can connect Google Workspace (Gmail, Drive, Docs, Calendar) for deeper integration. Your agents can deliver drafts directly to your Drive, send summaries to your Gmail, and schedule posts via Calendar. All optional and guided.
What happens after the first week?
After your first week at $7, the Chat + Pipeline plan bills at $27/mo. You can cancel any time from your Whop dashboard before the billing date and you won't be charged. Your agent environment stays live for 30 days after cancellation so you can grab your content and voice profile before it's removed.
